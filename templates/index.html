As Parent As School

All Inclusive Debt Management System For Schools

Yobai is a web application that will be designed primarily for school proprietors of primary and secondary schools in Abeokuta in Ogun state, Nigeria
to enable them identify debtors before granting them admission into their schools.
This platform will also give access to parents or guardians to enable them challenge their debt status or
make comments on the posts relating to them in the event that they were wrongly tagged as debtors.
This idea is borne out of the need to assist school owners in tracking potential debtors
and admitting only those cleared of all debts into their institutions.
